Docs - Lists

Home / Doc / Docs - Lists

We have provided five styles of content list

  • .list-simple - no bullets list
  • .list-alphabet - lists with alphabet
  • .list-decimal - lists with numeric
  • .list-bullet - list with bullet square
  • .list-text- list with title & list without title

List Simple No bullet

  • Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet

  • Consectetuer adipiscing elit

  • Aenean commodo ligula eget dolor

  • Cum sociis natoque penatibus et

          
    <!-- list no bullet -->
